HealthBits is a new company with a major goal.

Our vision comes from the perspective and experience of our founders, who spent decades working at the intersection of direct patient care, healthcare processes, healthcare reform, and cutting edge sensing and computing technology.

It’s the recognition of need in patient care and the promise of new technologies that drives us. The founding team at Healthbits is comprised of M.D.’s, Ph.D.’s, and engineers with practitioner, industry, and research knowledge. Serving at university appointments, as directors in major hospital systems, and as senior engineers, we established numerous patents and published on this issue for years, and now we’re excited to bring this vision to fruition.
